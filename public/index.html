Why "Lattice Governance"?

Unlike linear compliance checklists, our lattice governance framework maps interconnected regulatory dependencies across all authorities. A zoning change affects ESMA licensing, which impacts Civil Defence requirements, which influences DEWA capacity planning. We model these cross-dependencies as a governance lattice to identify cascade risks that linear approaches miss. Our parent company Taurus AI applies the same quantum-safe analytical rigor from financial services to property due diligence.
