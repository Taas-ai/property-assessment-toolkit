FAQ

How does Phase 2 differ from Phase 1?
Phase 2 adds physical site visit, market analysis, legal verification, environmental screening, and financial modeling. The report is 25-30 pages vs 8 pages, with 50+ pages of appendices.
Can I skip Phase 1 and go directly to Phase 2?
Yes, but we recommend Phase 1 first as a screening tool. If you already know the property is worth investigating, Phase 2 is the right choice.
Is the site visit included in the price?
Yes. One comprehensive site visit is included. Additional visits (e.g., for construction monitoring) can be arranged separately.
